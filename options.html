CARE THAT WORKS FOR YOU

This platform is made to schedule a quick and convenient time to have a caretaker check on an elderly person.

Types of care includes ensuring medication is refilled, helping replace a hard to open bottle with an easier one, cleaning, basic check ins, and more. This provides families with a cheaper and more efficient way to ensure their elderly person is not facing troubles with their medication. Conventional services will often require caretakers to stay for longer periods than may really be needed, which could be too expensive. Newer online services try to provide more flexibility, but are found to be very time consuming when it comes to searching for adequate and reasonable care.
